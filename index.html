The tool reduced the lead processing time from 2 weeks to less than a day.

Side Projects
Staying true to my passion, I’ve started new side projects every time I had some time to spare. This habit gave birth to 3 interesting ‘experiments’.
Experiments
  • Shopper.ma is a tool that scraps e-commerce websites to build an up-to-date database of all products on sale online in Morocco. The result was a fun and useful way to discover new products and compare prices. Technology used: Java Servlets, Crawler4J, JQuery single-page-app.
  • Trends is a recycling of Shopper into a news and gossip mobile app. By scrapping popular Facebook pages and sorting the posts by popularity bursts, Trends became extremely effective at sorting out the most popular news in real time. Technology used: Java Servlets, Cordova framework, Facebook graph API.
  • CROQS as stated in the aforementioned Call center project, CROQS is a tool that helped a call center operators’ qualify their leads through an automatic validation of contact information, scoring and dispatching/scheduling of calls. Technology used: Java Servlets, JQuery single page app, Google appengine.
